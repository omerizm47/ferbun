Frequently asked questions

Do I need an account?

No. Fêrbûn works fully offline and needs no sign-up. Your progress is saved on your device.

How do daily reminders work?

In Profile → Goals & Reminders, turn on the daily reminder and pick a time. It’s a local notification on your device — nothing is sent anywhere.

What is the Review feature?

As you practise flashcards, Fêrbûn remembers which words you find hard and brings them back at the right time using spaced repetition. Tap Review on the Home or Words tab when words are due.

How do I reset my progress?

Go to Profile → Reset All Progress. This permanently clears your data on the device.

Which languages are supported?

Fêrbûn teaches Kurmanji Kurdish, with explanations available in English and Turkish.
